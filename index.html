About SES

SES positions are published every four years by the GPO in a 'Plum Book'.

Each contains many (but not nearly all) SES members employed by Federal Government.

It does not contain the many SES members employed by States, private corporations or NGOs.

SES is a cadre of bureaucrats beholden to entities unknown.

About this Site

This site searches the 1996, 2004, 2008, 2012 & 2016 Plum Books published by the U.S. Government Publishing Office.

Plum Books are not published in a machine-readable format; the other Plum Books will be added if they are converted.

All Plumb Books were scraped from HTML or Text files; Plum Books from the GPO should remain your canonical source.

The dataset used can be exported from Google Fusion Tables.
